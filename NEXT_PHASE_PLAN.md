## Content Templates & Standards

### Page Structure Standard
All new pages should follow this structure:
- Hero section (H1, value prop, CTA)
- Introduction (150-200 words)
- Main content (sections with H2/H3)
- FAQ section (4-6 questions)
- CTA section (Get Quote / Contact)
- Schema markup (Service, Article, FAQ, etc.)

### SEO Checklist
- [ ] Unique title tag (50-60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars)
- [ ] H1 tag with primary keyword
- [ ] H2/H3 subheadings with semantic keywords
- [ ] Internal links to related pages (3-5 minimum)
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources
- [ ] Image alt text
- [ ] Schema markup
- [ ] Mobile-responsive
- [ ] Page load speed < 3s

### Content Quality Standards
- **Word Count Minimum:**
  - Service pages: 1,500 words
  - Industry pages: 1,200 words
  - Blog posts: 1,000 words
  - Guides: 2,000 words
  - Legal pages: 800 words

- **Readability:**
  - Grade 8-10 reading level
  - Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
  - Bullet points for scanability
  - Subheadings every 200-300 words

- **Keyword Density:**
  - Primary keyword: 1-2%
  - Semantic keywords: Natural inclusion
  - No keyword stuffing
